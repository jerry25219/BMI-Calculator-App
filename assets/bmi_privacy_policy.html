BMI Calculator Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the BMI Calculator app handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The BMI Calculator app is designed with privacy as a priority. We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • We do not collect any personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device content
  • We do not track your browsing history or activities in other apps
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not collect device information or unique identifiers

2. Local Data Storage

The BMI Calculator app only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Height, weight, and age data you input
  • BMI calculation history
  • App preferences and settings

Important: This data is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. It is never transmitted to our servers, cloud storage, or any third parties. We have no ability to access this data.

3. How We Use Your Data

The locally stored data is only used for:

  • Providing BMI calculation functionality
  • Displaying your calculation history
  • Restoring your settings when you reopen the app
  • Providing personalized health recommendations (based on your BMI results)

All calculations and data processing occur locally on your device without any network transmission.

4. Data Security

Since your data is only stored locally on your device, its security is protected by your device's security mechanisms. We recommend:

  • Setting up a passcode or biometric protection for your device
  • Keeping your device's operating system and apps updated
  • Not lending your device to untrusted individuals

5. Third-Party Services

The BMI Calculator app:

  • Does not integrate with any third-party analytics services
  • Does not contain any third-party advertising frameworks
  • Does not use any social media plugins
  • Does not utilize any cloud services for data storage

Our app functions completely independently without relying on external services.

6. App Permissions

The BMI Calculator app requires minimal permissions to function:

  • No location permissions
  • No camera or microphone access
  • No contacts or calendar access
  • No photo library or file system access (except for the app's own data storage)
  • No network connectivity required (the app can function completely offline)

7. Data Deletion

If you wish to delete all data stored by the app, you can:

  • Use the "Clear Data" function within the app (if provided)
  • Uninstall the application (this will remove all local data associated with the app)

8. Children's Privacy

The BMI Calculator app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will ensure the information is deleted.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and will be available in the app. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

10. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

This Privacy Policy complies with global privacy laws and regulations, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • App Store Review Guidelines
  • Huawei AppGallery Review Guidelines
